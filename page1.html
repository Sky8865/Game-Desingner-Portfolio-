Chaos Course

Step into the fiery, adrenaline-pumping world of Chaos Course, an exhilarating obstacle course game created with the cutting-edge power of Unreal Engine 5.

In Chaos Course, your mission is simple yet perilous: navigate through a series of challenging levels filled with relentless hazards, including blazing fires and cascading fireballs. Your ultimate goal is to collect all the scattered orbs in each level to advance and eventually conquer the course.

Key Features:

1. Intense Obstacle Challenges : Face a variety of obstacles designed to test your agility and strategic thinking.
2. Orb Collection Mechanics : Collect all the orbs scattered throughout each level to complete the stage.
3. Health Management : Keep an eye on your health bar, which depletes each time you encounter fire or get hit by a fireball. Strategize your movements to minimize damage and maximize your chances of survival.

Aim Master

Aim Master is an exhilarating target shooting game developed in Unreal Engine 5, where precision and speed are your ultimate allies. Immerse yourself in a dynamic and visually stunning environment, meticulously crafted using the latest advancements in UE5. As the clock ticks down, your mission is to shoot down all targets before time runs out, testing your marksmanship and reflexes to their limits..

Key Features:

1. Advanced Shooting Physics : Feel the authenticity with every shot, thanks to precise shooting mechanics and realistic muzzle behavior.
2. Destructible Targets : Witness spectacular destruction as targets break into pieces upon being hit, powered by cutting-edge physics simulations.
3. Time Challenge : Race against the clock in a thrilling countdown, where each second is precious and every target counts.
4. Intuitive Fire Logic : Enjoy seamless gameplay with expertly implemented fire logic, ensuring smooth and responsive shooting action.
5. Comprehensive Blueprint Programming : Experience a game built with the power of Unreal Engine's Blueprints, featuring robust systems for firing, timing, and target destruction.
